### Rubicon Fellowship
**Financialization of Rental Housing: A Historical Comparative Global Study**
***Dutch Research Council (NWO), 2024***
**Summary:** This project traces the rise of rental housing financialization across OECD countries and emerging economies. Hosted at Freie Universität Berlin, it examines how institutional investors enter local rental markets, how they interact with governments, and what this means for tenants' affordability and security. The goal is to inform regulation that protects tenants while accounting for national contexts.
